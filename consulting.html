WHY AN INDEPENDENT DIVISION

Advice that isn't tied to a contract

Ferranti Consulting operates as its own division precisely so its findings aren't shaped by who ends up winning the EPC work — including Ferranti Power. That separation is the product.

Most engineering advice comes bundled with a contractor's proposal — which means the study is written by the party with the most to gain from a "yes." Ferranti Consulting is built the other way around: engaged on its own scope, paid for its judgment, and free to recommend a design change, a different vendor, or that a project shouldn't proceed at all.

That independence is what lenders and boards are actually paying for when they commission a feasibility study or a technical due diligence report — a second opinion that doesn't quietly become a sales pitch.

Where it's useful, Ferranti Consulting works alongside Ferranti Power on the same asset — running the front-end study, then staying on as owner's engineer while Power executes. Where it's not, it works entirely on its own, reviewing a design that another contractor will build, or auditing a plant Ferranti Group had no hand in constructing.

Either way, the same engineers who've stood on live sites for three decades are the ones writing the report.

SERVICES

Engineering consultation, by stage

Feasibility & FEED

Technical and economic feasibility studies, front-end engineering design and concept selection — the analysis that decides whether, and how, a project should move forward.

Owner's Engineer

Independent oversight of a contractor's design and construction on the owner's behalf — reviewing drawings, verifying compliance and protecting the owner's technical interests through delivery.

Plant Audits & Efficiency Reviews

Post-commissioning audits of electrical and mechanical systems — identifying energy losses, ageing equipment risk and upgrade opportunities in an operating plant.

Safety & Compliance Studies

HAZOP reviews, electrical safety audits and regulatory compliance verification for facilities in power, process and industrial sectors.

Technical Due Diligence

Independent technical review for investors and lenders evaluating an acquisition, financing decision, or existing asset's condition.

Automation & Digital Strategy

Advisory on industrial automation, IoT instrumentation and digital-twin readiness for plants looking to modernise controls.
